# Ledgerly Project Summary

## Elevator Pitch

Ledgerly is a modern, mobile-first Personal Lending & Relationship Ledger built with Laravel 13 and React.

Rather than tracking isolated debts, Ledgerly helps users manage long-term financial relationships through a unified timeline of loans, repayments, reminders, notes, and financial activities.
